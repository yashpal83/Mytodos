Welcome to My Todos app.

This is the Very Simple and Easy to use Todo app.

It's very simple and easy to add your Daily Todos and Todo's Description here.

Below are the some Simple features of this Simple Todo app:

  • You can Add your daily TODOS and Todo's Description here.
  • When you will add any Todo and Todo's Description it will automatically add the current Date and time of that moment.
  • And according to your convenience you can Update any Todo's Title and Description.
  • The interesting thing in this app is, when you will Update any Todo's Title and Description it will automatically Update the current Date and Time of that Particular Todo.
  • You can search your Todos in the Search bar. You can search it by Todo's Title or by Todo's Description or even by Todo's Date and Time.
  • And if you want to Delete any Todo, you can do that as well.
  • The only thing you have to do is to create your account and Login. And you are ready to go!
  • If you have any suggestions or any query, Please feel free to Contact us.
